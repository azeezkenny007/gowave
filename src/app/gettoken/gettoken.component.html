HEDERA TOKEN

To host a game, you need to set the HBAR you want to reward the winner, this means you need to get the Hedera Token(HBAR) to do this.

Step 1: Create Hedera Portal Profile (Faucet)

Visit the Hedera portal to create your Hedera Testnet account and follow the instructions


Once you have completed the instructions, you will receive a Hedera Testnet account ID (0.0.x) and your private/public key pair on your testnet page. You will need to copy over your account ID and the DER-encoded private key information when you set up your account.

Note: Your Hedera Testnet account will be credited with 10,000 test HBAR upon creation that can only be utilized on the Hedera test network. Your balance will be topped up daily to 10,000 test HBAR when you use your funds.
